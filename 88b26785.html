I am Yann Ics, a sonic artist working with music as a concept.

My work mainly explores the potential of the interpreter(s) in a given situation.

Until now, I mainly experimented with music through score and quadraphonic installation.

The music I produced is not tonal, even if sometimes I used scales or borrowed melodies, and can be depicted more like algorithmic processes, which I mostly related in my writing named GSA : https://github.com/yannics/GSA/blob/master/gsa.pdf

Most of the processes generate abstract music which can be – according to the situation – felt like a soundscape, immersive sound experience, or other feelings that cannot be expressed objectively.

I occasionally produced music for short films and collaborated with storytellers.

I also experimented new approach for practicing music in a group with about ten participants : https://www.academia.edu/94964990
